About Himalaya Bhawan

Himalaya Bhawan is one of the newest hostels of IIT Roorkee, designed to offer a modern, comfortable, and inclusive living environment for students. Established in 2023, the Bhawan reflects a blend of contemporary infrastructure and community-oriented spaces.

We house students across multiple floors and are equipped with essential facilities including a dining mess, canteen, common recreational areas, laundry, and high-speed wired internet connectivity. Our focus is on convenience, sustainability, and student well-being.

Our Essential Facilities

Mess

Hygienic and well-balanced meals, supervised by the Warden. Separate vegetarian and non-vegetarian arrangements.

Service: Daily | Type: Veg & Non-Veg

Canteen

Convenient food facility offering a wide variety of snacks and drinks at reasonable prices.

Day Timings: 2:00 pm – 2:00 am

Study Room

A quiet and focused environment designed for individual study and group discussions.

Availability: Open daily for residents

Laundry

Dedicated laundry facility to support a structured and time-saving washing process. Paid dhobi service available.

Shift 1: 8am-2pm (Tues closed)

Guest Rooms

Comfortable accommodation for visitors and officials. Both AC and Non-AC rooms are available.

Tariff: ₹500 per day

Salon

Provides basic grooming and personal care services in a hygienic environment for resident convenience.

Timings: 10:00 am – 7:00 pm

Stationery

Provides access to commonly required writing and study-related supplies conveniently within the Bhawan premises.

Morning: 9:00 am – 2:00 pm

Juice Shop

Offers refreshing beverage options, fresh juices, and light refreshments for a quick and healthy alternative.

Timings: 11:00 am – 6:00 pm

Visitors Room

A comfortable, well-furnished, and air-conditioned space for residents to host their guests.

Access: As per hostel guidelines
